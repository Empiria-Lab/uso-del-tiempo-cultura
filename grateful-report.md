We used R v. 4.5.0 (R Core Team 2025) and the following R packages: brms
v. 2.23.0 (Bürkner 2017, 2018, 2021), fmsb v. 0.7.6 (Nakazawa 2024),
ggrepel v. 0.9.6 (Slowikowski 2024), lmtest v. 0.9.40 (Zeileis and
Hothorn 2002), performance v. 0.15.3 (Lüdecke et al. 2021), pins v.
1.4.1 (Silge, Wickham, and Luraschi 2025), plm v. 2.6.6 (Croissant and
Millo 2008, 2018; Millo 2017), renv v. 1.1.5 (Ushey and Wickham 2025),
scales v. 1.3.0 (Wickham, Pedersen, and Seidel 2023), sjPlot v. 2.8.17
(Lüdecke 2024), stargazer v. 5.2.3 (Hlavac 2022), survey v. 4.4.8
(Lumley 2004, 2010, 2024), tidyverse v. 2.0.0 (Wickham et al. 2019).
